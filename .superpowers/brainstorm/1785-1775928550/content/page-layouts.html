Navigation between destinations

How do users switch between the 3-5 destinations?

A

Tab bar at top

Fixed tabs showing destination names: "Bansko | Nuwara Eliya | Valle Nevado". Click to jump. Always visible.

B

Dot indicators + swipe (Recommended)

Small dots at top showing which destination you're on. Swipe left/right or use arrow keys. Minimal, clean, magazine-like.

C

Side drawer with thumbnails

Hidden sidebar that slides out, showing poster thumbnails for each destination. Tap to jump. More visual but adds complexity.
