A. Vertical Flip (Scroll-snap)

Scroll down to flip pages. Each page is full viewport height. CSS scroll-snap gives that satisfying page-lock feel. Simple, works everywhere, mobile-friendly.

B. Horizontal Book (3D page flip)

Like an actual book — two-page spreads with 3D CSS page-turn animations. Poster on left, content on right. Impressive but complex, may feel gimmicky on mobile.

C. Hybrid: Horizontal destinations + Vertical pages (Recommended)

Swipe left/right to switch between destinations. Within each destination, scroll down through full-screen pages (poster → facts → itinerary → tips). Best of both worlds — feels like flipping through a magazine. Tab bar or dots at top for destination navigation.
